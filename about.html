About us!

Our mission is to create cutting-edge digital lost-and-found software tailored to the unique needs of the deaf community. Our innovative platform streamlines the process of reporting and locating missing items, offering a game-changing solution for deaf users. We recognize the challenges that deaf individuals face when attempting to recover their lost belongings, often requiring cumbersome interactions with individuals who may not understand sign language. To address this issue, our software empowers users to provide detailed descriptions of their lost items. The system then employs sophisticated matching algorithms to scour our extensive database for products that closely align with the description. When a potential match is identified, with a remarkable accuracy rate of 80% or higher, the individual who reported the lost item receives a notification. This notification not only informs them of the potential match but also includes information about the likely location where the item was misplaced. If the user recognizes this location as a possible loss site, they are guided through a series of description-based questions to unlock a secure passcode. This passcode opens access to a secure locker, housing their missing item. Our comprehensive solution not only simplifies the reporting process and significantly enhances the likelihood of recovering lost possessions, but it also prioritizes efficiency and user-friendliness. We are dedicated to empowering the deaf community and revolutionizing the way they retrieve their lost items, eliminating language barriers and ensuring a seamless and effective experience.
